A weather app is a software application designed to provide users with real-time and forecasted weather information for a specific location or multiple locations. These apps use data from various sources, such as meteorological agencies, satellites, and weather stations, to deliver accurate and up-to-date weather information. Here are some common features you might find in a weather app:
Current Conditions: Displaying the current temperature, humidity, wind speed, and other relevant weather conditions for a specific location.
Forecast: Providing a multi-day forecast, typically ranging from a few days to a week, with information on expected temperatures, precipitation, and wind conditions.
Hourly Forecast: Breaking down the day into hourly segments to show how the weather is expected to change throughout the day.
Location-based Services: Allowing users to add and save multiple locations, enabling them to check the weather for different places.
Weather Widgets: Providing widgets for quick access to weather information directly from the home screen of a user's device.
Sunrise and Sunset Times: Displaying the times for sunrise and sunset in a given location.
